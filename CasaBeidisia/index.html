    • CASA BEIDISIA, UNPARALLELED LIVING IN COASTAL MEXICO

    GATED PRIVACY ON THE SHORES OF BANDERAS BAY WITHIN PUNTA DE MITA

    A PRESTIGIOUS OCEANFRONT ADDRESS AND INCOMPARABLE LUXURY

    Beidisia, the Afrikaans term meaning by the sea; this luxury beachfront estate perfectly marries the elements of space, service and serenity.

    Situated in the Ranchos Estates Punta Mita on the bay of Banderas (7th largest bay in the world). The estate is ideally placed 35 feet above a swimmable beach allows for optimal privacy and impressive vistas. The house leaves guests with little reason to leave, but amenities such as 2 private golf courses, tennis courts and 2 private beach clubs await within this gated luxury resort as do the famed Four Seasons and St. Regis.
